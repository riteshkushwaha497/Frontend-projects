OUR VISION

e-Entrepreneurship Cell, RGPV is a non-profit organization that believes entrepreneurship is pivotal for our country's development. It was established with the purpose of creating, fostering, and promoting the spirit of developing a culture of entrepreneurship in our surroundings. The team here works to encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their venture and dispel the fear of starting a startup. We guide young entrepreneurs and technopreneurs in their journey from campus to incubators and support them in transforming their budding ideas into successful startups.

SKILL DEVELOPMENT

“The future belongs to those who learn more skills and combine them in creative ways.”

An idea without implementation is just a dream. To achieve it one requires to put the effort in the right direction. These efforts can only be valuable if you have the skills needed to achieve your dream. E-cell RGPV not only provides a platform for young minds but also helps eager souls to learn new skills and develop the required entrepreneurial mindset needed to tackle the hurdles. E-cell RGPV gives an immense emphasis on skill-building and identifying and eliminating the visible skill gaps thereby honing and nurturing a candidate's potentials and aptitude to its optimum.

INITIATIVES

To fulfill our vision to build an ecosystem where networking and entrepreneurial mindset are encouraged, we have conceptualized and are successfully running the following initiatives to help future entrepreneurs in their entrepreneurial journey.

Alumunus Entrepreneurship

An initiative by E-Cell in association with Alumni Association that organizes Startup Master sessions that are highly enriching and enlightening for entrepreneurs which are graced by many famous personalities.

e - Mentoring Program

E-Mentoring Program is an initiative of E-CELL RGPV helping which helps budding ventures turn their dream into reality, under this program we provide assistance and guidance to water blooming startup ideas.

Startup Internship Program

Startup Internship Program (SIP) strives for developing an entrepreneurial streak among campus students by helping them acquire an internship in the most spirited and exciting startups running around.

Campus outreach Program

The campus ambassador program is an initiative by E-Cell RGPV to reach out to all the colleges across India intended to incorporate entrepreneurial spirit in students and foster an entrepreneurial mindset.

Rural Entrepreneurship

An initiative where we promote the potential and creative entrepreneurial ideas from various sections of society and present them to the vast network of entrepreneurs and investors, spotlighting the unique talents.

Entrepreneurship Awareness camp

An initiative that provides the enthusiasts with information about entrepreneurship culture within different campuses and breaking the ice of unawareness between the potential entrepreneurs and their success.

Startup Incubation

An initiative in which a club is formed for ignited, restless and creative minds to share their perspectives, thoughts, and knowledge with their fellow members and entrepreneurs a give some, take some place.
